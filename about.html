design stuff

what I consider UX design slightly differs from what you see on dribbble and popular UX design blogs (which is awesome, but not what I do). I’m honestly not good at making “gorgeous interfaces” and if you’re looking for that, I’m not really your person. My focus is on complex data organization, information-dense interfaces, and design based problem-solving. I also type a lot.
