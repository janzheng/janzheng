Pay-As-You-Go Billing Program Disclosure Statement Addendum

This Pay-As-You-Go Billing Program Disclosure Statement Addendum (“Addendum”) outlines differences in the Pay-As-You-Go Billing Program from the terms outlined previously in the Residential Disclosure Statement. All other Residential Disclosure Statement terms apply to the Pay-As-You-Go Billing Program and the Residential Terms and Conditions of Service apply in their entirety.

Billing and Payment: Gas South will mail your first Pay-As-You-Go bill within one week of your service start date. Thereafter, you will be billed based on your meter reading schedule set by AGL. The bill may contain AGL base charges, gas charges for the prior billing period, projected gas charges for the next 30 days, and a Gas South customer service fee. Each bill will also include an adjustment reflecting the difference between the prior period’s projected charges and the actual charges incurred. Your bill will also include all applicable federal, state and local taxes and charges. Your bill may also include adjustments related to previous billing errors, meter read errors, miscalculation of taxes or other errors or omissions as current laws and regulations permit. Early termination will not affect the charges imposed by AGL.

Budget Bill Payment Plan: The Budget Bill Payment Plan is not available to customers enrolled in the Pay-As-You-Go Billing Program.

Deposits: Gas South will not require a deposit from customers who enroll in the Pay-As-You-Go Billing Program.

Late Payment, Nonpayment, Disconnection and Collection: An accelerated disconnection timeline is a condition of Gas South’s Pay-As-You-Go Billing Program, whereby gas service will be subject to disconnection if a bill is not paid in full within at least twenty-five (25) days after the date the bill is mailed. Gas South will deliver a written notice of the proposed disconnection at least fifteen (15) days prior to the date of disconnection. This notice will be included in each bill. Before a request is made to AGL , Gas South will make available at least one (1) reasonable payment arrangement in writing. Gas service will not be disconnected by Gas South if it is not your current marketer or for nonpayment of a bill that was not sent to you in a timely manner or for any portions of a bill resulting from a billing dispute. At least two (2) days prior to the proposed disconnection, Gas South will make a good-faith effort to contact you. You remain liable for all charges incurred until such time as the gas service is disconnected. Ten (10) days after your service is disconnected, your account may be sent to a third-party collection agency for further action. If you ask to have service reconnected, Gas South will require you to pay reconnect charges and provide payment for any outstanding balances. You may also be charged a reconnection fee of no more than $25.00, in addition to any charges assessed by AGL.
