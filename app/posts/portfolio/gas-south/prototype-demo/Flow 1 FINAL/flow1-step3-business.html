Georgia Business Fixed Rate Plans:

     
A fixed rate plan provides a consistent rate per therm over the contract period. All business rate plans are subject to a monthly Customer Service Fee, Interstate Pipeline Charges, taxes and Atlanta Gas Light Company (AGLC) charges. This rate plan is subject to a $300 early contract cancellation fee if terminated before contract expires. More Info

Georgia Business Variable Rate Plan:

       
This business rate plan fluctuates month to month with the natural gas market. All business rate plans are subject to a monthly Customer Service Fee, Interstate Pipeline Charges, taxes and Atlanta Gas Light Company (AGLC) charges. There is no contract and you can change this rate plan anytime without penalty. More Info

Eligibility is subject to consumption, location, and credit approval.
